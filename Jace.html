Jace Beleren

About Jace

A human Planeswalker from Vryn who is adept with blue magic. He is the Living Guildpact of Ravnica and a founding member of the Gatewatch.

Trivia About Jace

  1. Jace's unique gift of telepathy manifested very early in his life
  2. Jace was apprenticed to a Sphinx to hone his magic and illusions
  3. Jace lived his life in Ravnica in relative luxury by blackmailing the wealthy with their darkest secrets
  4. Jace unwittingly released of the Eldrazi Titans from their prison in Zendikar
